About Me

I am Xuanming Liu (刘轩铭), a senior high school student in Nanjing, Jiangsu, China. My research interests lie in Generative AI and Efficient Multimodal Models.

My research focus is on the exploration of efficient generative models, with an emphasis on innovative methods that go beyond the traditional expansion paradigm. The ultimate goal is to construct an efficient and interactive world model by integrating rapid pattern matching with cautious reasoning, and to promote real-time applications ranging from on-device creative assistants to scalable intelligent agent systems.
